Welcome to Lanchonete Delícias do Lanche

Our History

Lanchonete Delícias do Lanche was founded in 2005 by two friends who were passionate about food and the idea of ​​creating a welcoming space where people can enjoy delicious and succulent snacks.

Since then, we have dedicated ourselves to offering our customers a variety of Chinese options, made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Our goal is to provide a satisfying dining experience with every visit.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with an exceptional gastronomic experience, offering delicious snacks, refreshing juices and top quality service. We are committed to promoting the satisfaction and well-being of our customers in a welcoming and relaxed environment.


  • Variety

    Our snack bar offers a wide variety of snacks, from classic burgers to vegetarian and vegan options, for all tastes and preferences.


  • Freshness

    We use fresh, carefully selected ingredients in all our preparations, guaranteeing the flavor and quality of our snacks.


  • Flavor

    Our snacks are prepared with great care and dedication, always seeking to provide an explosion of flavors in each bite.


  • Service

    Our team is committed to providing friendly and efficient service, ensuring a pleasant and satisfactory experience for all our customers.
