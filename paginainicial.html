Welcome to Snack Bar and Juices

Our History

Our snack bar, Lanches e Jucos, started in 2005 with a small juice stand at a local fair. Over time, we noticed the growing demand for quick and healthy snacks, and expanded our services to include a variety of sandwiches, salads and fresh juices.

Over the years, we've built a community of loyal customers who enjoy our fresh, flavorful food made with high-quality, local ingredients. Our commitment to culinary excellence and customer service has made us a leader in the region, and we continue to grow and innovate to meet the needs of our customers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with a delicious and healthy gastronomic experience, offering fresh and nutritious food options at affordable prices. We are committed to promoting a balanced and sustainable lifestyle, valuing the health and well-being of our customers and the community at large.

  • Variety

    We offer a wide variety of snacks, including sandwiches, wraps, salads and vegetarian options, to suit all tastes and preferences.

  • Freshness

    Our ingredients are fresh and carefully selected, guaranteeing the quality and flavor of our products.

  • Health

    We prioritize the health and well-being of our customers, offering nutritious and balanced food options, without compromising taste.

  • Satisfaction

    Our goal is to provide a satisfying and memorable dining experience for each customer who visits our restaurant.
